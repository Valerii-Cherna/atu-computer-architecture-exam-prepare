3) How is your secondary storage organized


-----------------------------------
Secondary storage:
  - is non-volatile storage used for long-term data storage;
  - Data on secondary storage is typically organized into files and directories;

  - The organization of secondary storage involves file systems;
  - A file system is a method used by an OS to organize and store data on a storage device;
  - It manages how data is stored, retrieved, and organized into files and directories;

  - NFTS(Windows);
  - ext4(Linux);
  - APFS(macOS);
