4) ESD


-----------------------------------
Electrostatic discharge(ESD):
  - is the transfer of electrons from one object to another;
  - Most electronic computer devices are sensitive to changes at less than 1.5 volts;
  - When you work on the internal components of your computer;
  - you may damage the components in the computer via ESD, and not even feel it;
