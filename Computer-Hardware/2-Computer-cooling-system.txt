2) Computer cooling system


-----------------------------------
Computer cooling system:
  - is an active or passive cooling system designed to dissipate excessive heat generated through a PC;

Types:
  1) Fans:
    - an active cooling system;
    - they use energy to run, thus enabling the air to circulate;

  2) Heat sinks:
    - a passive cooling system;
    - their function is to dissipate the heat from the element they have been fixed to;
